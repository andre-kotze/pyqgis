ABOUT IMDH PHOTO RENAME

IMDH PHOTO RENAME sorts and renames sample photos according to the drill start times logged by geologists.
JPGs are detected in the "To be renamed" folder of the selected concession. The screenlog is then read and filtered to the date range of the photos.
For support and development needs please get in touch with AKO GeoT. Feedback and suggestions are welcome.
